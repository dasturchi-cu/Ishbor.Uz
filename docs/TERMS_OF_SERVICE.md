## 7. Orders, contracts, and delivery

### 7.1 Order lifecycle

Standard order flow:

```
pending → active → delivered → completed
                    ↓
                 disputed
```

- **Pending:** Order created; awaiting payment
- **Active:** Payment secured in escrow; work in progress
- **Delivered:** Freelancer submitted deliverables
- **Completed:** Employer confirmed; escrow released
- **Disputed:** Either party opened a dispute; funds frozen pending resolution

### 7.2 Milestones

Contracts may include milestones. Each milestone may have independent escrow release upon Employer approval.

### 7.3 Acceptance and auto-release

If Employer does not respond within **[X]** days of delivery, escrow may auto-release to Freelancer per platform rules displayed at order creation. Employers should review delivery promptly.

### 7.4 Cancellations

Cancellation rules depend on order status:

| Status | Rule |
|--------|------|
| Pending (unpaid) | Either party may cancel freely |
| Active | Requires mutual agreement or dispute |
| Delivered | Employer may accept or dispute; not unilaterally cancel |
| Completed | No cancellation; refund only via dispute |
